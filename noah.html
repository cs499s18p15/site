Noah's Weekly Updates


UPDATE: Mar. 4, 2018

We met with Toyota this past week and were told we would need to change our database. This will mean our project's front-end progress has stopped and we are all hands on deck on migrating to a different database. Toyota gave us a choice between MS SQL (greatly preferred), MySQL, and PostreSQL. We have come to a mutual decision that MySQL will likely be the best option because three team members (myself included) will not be able to locally run the version of the MS SQL server Toyota uses, due to having Macbooks. We still need to get the go-ahead from our customer contact, we were unable to have our weekly meet with him this past Thursday because he was out sick. Beyond researching our database options and how to migrate our project to a new one, I also began to update the user scenarios section for this progress site. I also added a some of the nodemailer package functionality in order to send registering users a confirmation email. This feature's progress has now been temporarily halted because of the database issue.
