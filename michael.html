March 4th, 2018

This past week we met with Toyota at their facilities on Tuesday. We demonstrated the current user scrum tool and got user feedback. They liked what we have so far, but wanted to note specific requirements that were new. They requested the ability to search projects completion date and group. They also wanted the ability to measure projects by cost, quality, and timing. It was great to see the users using the proof of concept. We found out that Toyota does not support no SQL databases. So we need to migrate our data to mySQL. It will be a bit challenging. I suggested to our customer that we create a dev enviorment at their facility and train them how to pull from our master branch so that they could test and give us feedback. I returned to Toyota last Friday and spent 5 hours getting the dev enviorment setup and running and I learned that their boards show our user interface differently than expected. It should appear vertically, but it shows up horizontally. I'm not exactly sure why yet, but with more user testing it will become apparent.
